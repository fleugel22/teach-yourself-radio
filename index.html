I/Q signal primer

Understanding I/Q radio data

An interactive, hands-on introduction to how radio signals are stored as I/Q data and how individual signals are pulled back out of a wideband capture. Built for people meeting radios for the first time — every plot is a live algorithm you can drag.

Part 1
What lives inside an I/Q recording
The spinning arrow, many signals adding into one stream, the spectrum, and the bytes on disk.
Part 2
Pulling one signal out of the mix
Tune, filter, decimate — the digital downconverter — plus the FFT filter-bank shortcut.
I = in-phase · Q = quadrature. React is loaded from a CDN (esm.sh); the lesson code is local. Serve over http(s), not a file:// path.
