I/Q signal primer

Understanding I/Q radio data

A hands-on introduction to the groundwork of digital radio, how signals are modulated and stored as I/Q data, how individual signals are pulled out of a wideband capture, how a receiver turns them back into bits, and how multiple antennas carry parallel streams. Built for people meeting radios for the first time — every plot is a live algorithm you can drag.
